Privacy Policy — Petanque Master

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

Petanque Master is a browser-based game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Local Storage

The game uses your browser's localStorage to save your progress (scores, unlocked characters, settings). This data stays on your device and is never sent anywhere.

Third-Party Services

When played on third-party platforms (CrazyGames, Poki), those platforms may collect analytics data according to their own privacy policies. Please refer to their respective policies for details.

Cookies

The standalone version of the game does not use cookies. Third-party platforms may use cookies per their own policies.

Children's Privacy

Petanque Master is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
